Change Password

Users can change their password by clicking the profile icon and the Change Password item in the dropdown.

If you are on App Designer or Data Stream Designer, this will open Change Password in Subscription Manager, where you can change your password.

You are required to enter both your current password and your new password. You will need to enter your new password twice to confirm it. Finally, click on Save.
