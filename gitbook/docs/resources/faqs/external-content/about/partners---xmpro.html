Partners

XMPRO’s trusted partner ecosystem provides our customers with the expertise and technology to deploy highly impactful Event Intelligence applications faster than ever before.

Technology Partners

Our Technology Partner Program provides certification to products and services that successfully work with our platform. Compatible products including sensors, devices, hardware and software can be validated by XMPRO. Certified software is made available to clients through our Integration Library. Our growing network of Technology Partners make it possible for our customers to extend their Event Intelligence applications with certified components that speed up the development process and extend the scope of their solution.

Joint Solution Partners

Our Joint Solution Partners work with us to develop applications that typically focus on specific verticals or use cases. This model combines the capabilities of our application development platform with our partner’s technology and industry-specific expertise to create a packaged solution that delivers fast time to value. We also work with our partners to create a joint go-to-market strategy and actively promote the joint solution to our target audience.
