A Practical Framework to Turn IoT Technology Into Operational Capability

Posted on March 21, 2018 by xmpro

The Industrial Internet Consortium (IIC) published its 7th edition of the Journal of Innovation today.

XMPro’s CEO, Pieter van Schalkwyk, contributed A Practical Framework to Turn IoT Technology Into Operational Capability to this Digital Transformation-focused edition.

Pieter’s article provides a practical framework you can use to answer the following questions in your organization:

  1. How do we describe and agree on the key business outcomes that an IoT solution will deliver?
  2. How do we describe and agree on the business integration of heterogeneous machine-borne (IoT) data with existing business applications?
  3. How do we describe and agree on the integration of IoT into existing business workflows?
